23 October 2019

"Space on wheels" at VSSUT burla

As Neil deGrasse Tyson has said "Even the sky is not the limit " and Idea Innovation Cell proved it yet again. The event held on 23rd October "Space on wheels" at VSSUT burla was a huge success over 3K people came to see the marvelous creations by ISRO . Today's event was inaugurated by eminent scientist Arjun Sahu, ISRO and our honorable Professors Dr.Bhibuti Bhusan Pati, Dean Student Welfare Dr.Sudhanshu Shekar Das, Head of Innovation Centre Dr. Debadutta Mishra, Dean Academics Affair Dr. Sarat Kumar Swain, Dean PGS & R Prof.Prakash Chardra Swain,Technical Vice-President Dr. Harish Sahoo, and other faculty members from VSSUT. The event ended on a high note and it's success was a coalescence of enthusiasm towards space science from everyone who turned up to the occasion- students, faculty members, the localities & the collective and cooperative effort by our team. Each and every member of Idea & Innovation Cell is extremely grateful for the overwhelming response.
