A first-of-its-kind 2 Day Technical Workshop on Launch Vehicle Technology

A first-of-its-kind 2 Day Technical Workshop on Launch Vehicle Technology was organized on 25/04/19 - 26/04/19 to impart knowledge on Ground Station & Telemetry, Guidance & Control, Materials & propulsion, Pyro & separation systems, Range safety & precautions. It hosted following 11 eminent speakers from different centres of ISRO - Indian Space Research Organisation.
Sri Bhatt Tushar Shriram, PSCG, VSSC
Sri Rajendran S, STR Entity, VSSC
Sri MNNamboodiripad, DD, AVN, VSSC
Sri Narasimham JV, DD, GSNA, ISTRAC
Sri Ramkumar P, GM, MMA, VSSC
Sri K Poonginan, DD, LSSF, SHAR
Sri Rajesh Prabhudas Mohod, MVIT, VSSC
Sri Gopal Jee, DH, CGSE, VSSC
Sri Premdas M, ASMG, VSSC
Sri Baby Abraham, GD, ASOE, VSSC
Sri Jiwan Kumar Pandit, AD, OHRD, CBPO, ISRO-HQ
Our heartfelt thanks to the ISRO Officials who took their time out of their busy schedules to visit Burla and impart such unparalleled training to our team. Excitedly looking forward to future collaboration.
